About Galit

Twenty years.
One industry.
All in.

Galit started her career as a commercial designer for the Victoria's Secret division through Delta Galil, and spent two decades building deep expertise — and deeper relationships — across product development, factory sourcing, and supply chain management for intimates and activewear.

In 2017, she launched Miss Galit LLC to bring that experience directly to brands who need it most: the founders who are too smart to go it alone, and too independent to work with someone who doesn't actually care.
