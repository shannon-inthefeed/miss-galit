Her Story

She didn't just learn this industry. She built a career inside it.

Galit Wexler is an Israeli-American designer and product developer who started her career as a commercial designer for the Victoria's Secret division through Delta Galil — one of the most demanding intimates environments in the world. What she built there wasn't just experience. It was a network of factories, suppliers, and industry relationships that most consultants spend entire careers chasing.

For twenty years, she moved through product development, design, and business leadership — gaining the kind of knowledge that only comes from being inside rooms where the hard decisions get made. Bra construction. Fabric sourcing. Factory negotiations. Costing under pressure. She's done it all, at every scale.

In 2017, after heading the Delta Galil New York office, she made a decision: to stop building someone else's brand and start running alongside the founders who needed everything she'd learned. Miss Galit LLC was born.

Today she leads a hand-picked team out of New Jersey and New York. She's contributed to six registered patents in fashion and textiles, helped launch seven emerging brands, and made a point of staying small enough to be fast — and close enough to care.

"I'm a small vessel in a sea of cruise ships. I maneuver. I react. And I always have a Plan B already in mind."

Galit Wexler
